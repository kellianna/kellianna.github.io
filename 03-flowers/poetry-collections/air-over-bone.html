Air Over Bone


This offering of quiet poetry seeks to unite through the collective experience of heartbreak, redemption, and living in a modern tapestry that seems to be unraveling one sunset thread at a time.

Air Over Bone was my first collection of poetry, self published in 2018 as a print book that I have since decided to give it away for free as a PDF. I’m very proud of the poems in this collection of quiet poetry which seeks to unite through the collective experience of heartbreak, redemption, and living in a modern tapestry that seems to be unraveling one sunset thread at a time. 

Here you’ll find love. 

Here you’ll find anger. 

Here you’ll find anguish. 

But, maybe in the end, here you’ll find hope.

Poem Samples

Quote

You landed like a feather
on my empty page. So
soft and light that
my slightest exhale
moved you. My slight
movement grasped
you where you fell.
Inky hands held your
secrets safe, wrapped
tight in my mother tongue.

~ from the poem, Flightless

Quote

Suddenly knowing
how craving waits
in the mouth as
the flesh of a
juicy cantaloupe,
while hunger lives
deep in the belly
as the gnawing
of a bear at
a steel trap.

~ from the poem, Air Over Bone

Quote

Through the willingness of faith,
through the myths on the wind,
we rewrite these ancient ballads
to speak to the empty black
space between the stars
where we have always
sought our blessings.

~ from the poem, Sestina in Black
